Chocolate cookies recipe

This a simple chocolate cookie recipe, you should get some cakey, fluffy and really good cookies out of this, pretty quick recipe, it takes me about 1 hour or so to make, maybe a little less, depends on the day.

The part where I talk, or write, about how i got this recipe is at the bottom. Not wasting time here folks.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 5 spoons of butter or margarine (either one should work)
  • 2½ spoons of flour (just about, like, give or take a little)
  • ½ spoon of baking soda/yeast powder
    • (or something like that, i don't remember the actual name in english, but it's the one you use for cakes to grow.)
  • pieces of chocolate (optional, but very recommended [this is a chocolate cookie recipe after all.])
  • (optional) 1 table spoon of cocoa powder, or chocolate powder (or maybe more, go by you taste, again just whatever you have on hand.)

Preparing

  1. Pre-heat the oven at around 180°C

  2. Grease pan with butter/margarine and flour

  3. Mix sugar and butter/margarine until somewhat uniform

  4. Add eggs and mix until smooth

  5. Slowly add flour while mixing in between the adding, until not sticking to the bowl or hands. Here, after 2 cups or so of flour, you slow down on it, go mixing and adding the flour, that way you don't risk passing a good batter texture

  6. This is where you add the baking soda, make sure to mix it well

  7. If chosen, add the chocolate cocoa powder

  8. Add the chocolate pieces

  9. Bake at about 250°C for around 20 minutes, I prefer to let it cool for little bit inside the oven, but it should be good to take and let it cool outside of it.
