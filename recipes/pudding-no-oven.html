No oven pudding

This one is a no oven required pudding recipe, the whole thing shouldn't take more than 2 hours to make, and then many hours in the fridge, don't make it if you are in a rush.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 can of heavy cream (about the same amount of condensed milk)
  • 1 can of milk (same amount as the condensed milk)
  • 1 packet of unflavoured gelatine

Preparing

  1. Dump the condensed milk, heavy cream and milk into a mixer

  2. Prepare the unflavoured gelatine according to the instructions given by the brand

  3. Add the gelatine to mixer as well, and mix it until unified

  4. The sungar melt, add the sugar to a pan, and heat it up until golden

  5. Add the sugar to the bottom of the form while still hot, and on top add the milk mixture

  6. Refrigerate for about 6-8 hours

Banter

Whenever I decide to make pudding here I usually make it after 12h, and leave it in the fridge through the night, unforming it only the next day. Usually it lasts two, three days around here. It's good in the hot days to have something cool.
